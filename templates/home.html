Medical Diagnosis :
AI-powered systems to analyze user-reported symptoms and medical data to predict potential health conditions and recommend appropriate actions, such as specialist consultations or treatment options.
Medical Interaction :
involves real-time conversation where the AI collects symptoms, analyzes health data, provides possible diagnoses, suggests treatments, and answers medical queries, enhancing accessibility to healthcare.
Recommendation :
Recommends patients by analyzing their symptoms and medical history to suggest possible diagnoses, appropriate treatments, and referrals to specialists or nearby healthcare facilities based on their condition.
Q & A with Chatbot :
interacts with patients by providing accurate and context-aware responses to medical queries, explaining symptoms, conditions, treatments, and medical terms in an easy-to-understand manner.
